Hi Everyone!

I'm Louise! I'm a sophomore at Scripps College, and this is my website for my CS040 class at Claremont Mckenna College. This is a little about me!

Academics

I'm undeclared as of now, but am planning to major in either Spanish literature or anthropology. This begs the question: why comp sci? This class is about the polar opposite to everything else I've been taking, and that was kind of the point. I'm considering a comp sci or data science minor to balance out my strong humanities focus, and also, comp sci is one of those disciplines that will match with whatever your interests are. The overlap between language learning and learning how to code has already been very impressive to me!
